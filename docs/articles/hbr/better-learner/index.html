    4 Ways to become a better learner

    • Staying within your comfort zone is a good way to prepare for today, but it’s a terrible way to prepare for tomorrow.

    • Learning agility is the capacity for rapid, continuous learning from experience.

    • Agile learners are good at making connections across experiences, and they’re able to let go of perspectives or approaches that are no longer useful — in other words, they can unlearn things when novel solutions are required. People with this mindset tend to be oriented toward learning goals and open to new experiences.

    • A growing body of research shows that systematically reflecting on work experiences boosts learning significantly.
